BOARDERLINE

Ski & Snowboard Club

Boarderline Ski & Snowboard Club at James Madison University is a mountain lover's one stop shop for enjoying winter sports in the Valley! Our student-run organization focuses on creating a community of skiiers, boarders, and winter sport lovers. Wether you are a seasoned pro or just starting out on the mountain - all are welcome with Boarderline.
